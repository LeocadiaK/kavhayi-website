Annapolis

Annapolis is the capital city of the state of Maryland. It is located in Anne Arundel County on the Chesapeake Bay. It was founded in 1649 and incorporated in 1708.The 2020 census recorded the population of Annapolis as 40,812. 21.7% of the population in Annapolis is black, 49.4% is White, 0.7% is Native American, 2.5% is Asian and 14.5% according to the 2020 census. Hispanic and Latino residents of ay race made up 22.9% of the population. Annapolis is classified as urban and offers its residents an urban surbuban feel. The average household income of Annapolis households is $124,271 according to World Population Review.

Potomac

Potomac is one of the best places to live in Maryland. Potomac is an unicorporated community in Montgomery County that is classified as a suburb. The 2020 census placed the population of Potomac at 46,256. In 2010 the racial make up of Potomac was 75.8% white, 4.6% African American, 0.1% Native American, 15.9% Asian and 2.6% other races. Hispanic and Latino residents of any race were 6.4% of Potomac's population according to the Census Bureau.

Potomac is noted for its beauty and historical significance. It is one of the wealthiest surburbs in Maryland.The average household income in Potomac is $263,322 and the median household income sitsat $198,870 annually. The city of Potomac is named after the Potomac River also found in Maryland.

Baltimore

Baltimore City is the most populous city in Maryland. It is located in Baltimore County and is rich in history. Baltimore is also the largest city and economic centre in Maryland. Baltimore City is classified as urban. It was founded in 1729 and incorporated in 1797. As of the 2020 census the population in Baltimore metropolitan was 2,838,327. Baltimore has a large Carribean American population with the largest groups being Jamaicans and Trinidadians. The 2010 census revealed 63.7% of Baltimore's population as Black, 26.9% as White , 2.3% Asian, 4.2% Hispanic or Latino and 0.4% Native American and Alaska Native. In 2021, the median household income of Baltimore households was $54,124 and the average household income currently sits at $81,172 according to the US Census Bureau.
